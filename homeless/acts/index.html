Homeless in Hong Kong

Addressing the issue of Homeless in Hong Kong, we hope to tackle the issue as it is a main problem in Hong Kong.

So, what can we do about it?

What we have been doing

Throughout the process of addressing this issue, we have been hoping to raise awareness about this issue in Hong Kong. We know that the small group of us is absolutely not enough to solve a problem that matters the entire city, so we hope to bring as many people as possible to together to resolve this issue that has lasted for ages in Hong Kong.

What you can do about it

By raising awareness, we hope to bring you (yes, you, the person who's reading this right now) to help them out. There are many ways to help them out, like raising awareness (share this website!), donating to charities that helps people that are homeless, or even directly help them such as giving out meals to them or giving out blankets, clothes or other things that can potentially help them to survive. Some charities you can donate to includes:
St Barnabas Society and Home, Impact HK, Feeding Hong Kong, or Food Angel
