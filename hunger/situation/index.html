Hunger in Hong Kong

Addressing the issue of Hunger in Hong Kong, we hope to tackle the issue as it is a main problem in Hong Kong.

So, what is the situation?

Currrently...

In Hong Kong, there are currently hundreds of thousands of people that are in hunger, according to the Hong Kong Social and Welfare Department. With COVID-19 in the way, this issue is harder to solve as there are more people getting unemployed and not able to find a job.

It's not that big of a deal right?

These hundreds of thousands of people hunger could possibly be due to the only finnancial support of the family losing their job, which can be a really big deal as that could affect the development of a child.

What is the government doing about it?

The government is allowing people that are in need to apply for CSSA (Comprehensive Social Security Assisstant), which hopes to relieve the financial stress of individuals or families
